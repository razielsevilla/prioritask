PrioriTask Privacy Statement (Draft Final)

Effective date: 2026-03-28

PrioriTask stores assignment and settings data locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local.
PrioriTask does not transmit your task data to external servers.
PrioriTask does not use analytics or tracking SDKs.
PrioriTask does not collect personal identifiers, contacts, or browsing history.

Permissions used:
- storage: save your assignments and preferences.
- alarms: run scheduled reminder checks.
- notifications: show due/risk reminder alerts.

Data retention:
- Data remains on your local browser profile until you edit/delete tasks or remove extension data.
